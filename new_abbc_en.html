ABBC

ABBC is the exclusive currency that is used for making purchases.

Has ABBC purchase method been changed?

Yes, the ABBC purchase using Mileage(₩) has ended since Apr 30th, 2021.
The Buyaladdin team finished working on the integrations of ABBC coin payments on the shopping application. You can shop in the Buyaladdin app using ABBC.

How can I ‘Receive’ ABBC to Buyaladdin’s ABBC wallet?

You can receive your ABBC on other exchanges or personal wallets into the ABBC card within the Buyaladdin app.

Please send more than the minimum amount set by Buyaladdin. It will be difficult to receive the amount if you send less than the minimum amount.

Buyaladdin sets the values of ABBC based on real-time market prices. In addition, the market price may change continuously.

The maximum amount to receive of ABBC is unlimited. ABBC will show up to 8 decimal places.

You can purchase products in the shopping malls in Korea using ABBC.

Is there a user guide for ABBC ‘Receive’?

Yes, please refer to the following below.


Click the ‘Receive’ button on your ‘My Wallet’ page.

Check the Agreement related to the cryptocurrency and click the ‘Agree’ button.

Check the BTC, ETH, ABBC Agreement & User Guide and click the ‘Agree’ button.

Please set your Passcode if it is your first time using the ‘Receive’ function.

When you complete setting your Passcode, you will be given the BTC, ETH, ABBC wallet addresses. Please copy and store your wallet addresses.

Click the 'Receive' button once more on your ‘My Wallet’ page.

Select where to send it from on the ‘Request to Receive’ page. (Select ‘Wallet’ or ‘Exchange’)

Fill out the send date, send time, and the amount you want to send, and click the ‘OK’ button. (It will be difficult to receive the amount if you send less than the minimum amount.)

You will be automatically moved to the ‘Wallet QR’ page.

Send ABBC from the exchange or wallet you use referring to the ‘Wallet QR’ page.

The amount you sent will be displayed. Check the latest transaction on ‘My Wallet’ within Buyaladdin.

When the ‘Receive’ process is complete, the receive confirmation will be sent to your email registered in Buyaladdin or the notification in the app.

Is it possible to ‘Send’ ABBC to my wallet?

To ‘Send’ ABBC to other exchanges or personal wallets needs the Buyaladdin’s approval process.


When you request to ‘Send’, the transaction fee will be charged. Accordingly, if the amount you have is less than the total amount including the transaction fee, it will be processed.


When you request to ‘Send’, the transaction may take 24 to 48 hours due to the approval process.


After the ‘Send’ completion, the amount you requested will be sent to the wallet address you entered.

Is there a user guide for ‘Send’?

Yes, please refer to the following below.


Click the 'Send' button to send ABBC you have in Buyaladdin to your cryptocurrency wallet.

You can ‘Send’ ABBC according to Buyaladdin’s approval process. Click on the ‘Agree’ button on the approval pop-up page.

Enter the Wallet Address to send. (QR code is available.)

Enter the amount of ABBC. KRW will be automatically calculated and displayed.

Now, complete the email verification. Please enter your email address registered in Buyaladdin.

When you click the ‘Send’ button, the requested amount will be sent to the wallet address you entered.

Can I use the other currency after ‘Send’ request?

Yes, please note that when you request to ‘Send’ ABBC, the next ‘Send’ request will be unavailable until the previous request is complete. (The use of ‘Receive’ and other currency you have within the app is available.)

How can I use ABBC?

You can purchase products in the Korean shopping mall provided by Buyaladdin.
If you purchase products using ABBC, the amount of ABBC equivalent to the product will be automatically calculated and get displayed.
