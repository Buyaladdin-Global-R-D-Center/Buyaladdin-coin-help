How can I ABBC deposit in my Buyaladdin wallet?

With Buyaladdin, you can deposit ABBC (ABBC) from personal wallets or other exchanges directly into your ABBC card.
The value of ABBC is subject to real-time market prices and fluctuations.

You can deposit amounts as small as 0.00000001 ABBC, with up to 8 decimal places of visibility and no maximum limit. Once you deposit ABBC from your wallet, you can use the amount reflected on your ABBC card.

We recommend sending an amount greater than the minimum set by Buyaladdin to ensure the successful receipt of your requested amount.
Furthermore, you can purchase products in shopping malls located in Korea using ABBC.

What are the steps for ABBC deposit in Buyaladdin?

To deposit ABBC on Buyaladdin, follow these steps:


1. Click ‘Deposit’ on the ‘My Wallet’ page.

2. Agree to the related cryptocurrency agreement.

3. Agree to the ABBC agreement and user guide.

4. Set your passcode if it’s your first time using the ‘Deposit’ function.

5. After setting your passcode, you’ll be given your ABBC wallet address. Copy and store the master key.

6. Return to the ‘My Wallet’ page and click ‘Deposit’ again.

7. Choose whether to send from a wallet or exchange on the ‘Request Deposit’ page.

8. Enter the send date, send time, and amount to send. Note that sending less than the minimum amount may result in difficulty depositing the requested amount.

9. Click “Ok” to continue to the ‘Wallet QR’ page.

10. Deposit ABBC from your exchange or wallet using the wallet address provided on the ‘Wallet QR’ page.

11. The amount you sent will be displayed in the app. Check for the latest transaction on the ‘My Wallet’ page.

12. Once the ‘Deposit’ is complete, you’ll receive a confirmation via email or in-app notification

Can I withdraw ABBC from Buyaladdin to my personal wallet?

Yes, you can. To withdraw ABBC to other exchanges or personal wallets, you need to go through Buyaladdin's approval process, which may take more than 48 hours to complete.


Please note that a transaction fee will be charged when you make a 'Withdrawal' request. If the total amount you have is not sufficient to cover the transaction fee, the transaction may not be processed.


Once the 'Withdrawal' request is approved, the requested amount will be sent to the wallet address you entered.

What are the steps for withdrawing ABBC from Buyaladdin?

To withdraw ABBC from Buyaladdin to your cryptocurrency wallet, follow these steps:


1. Click the 'Withdrawal' button and go through Buyaladdin's approval process.

2. Click 'Continue' on the pop-up page.

3. Enter the wallet address manually or use the provided QR code.

4. You only can withdraw with the entered wallet address.

5. Enter the amount of ABBC. The dollar value will be automatically displayed.

6. Enter the Memo.

7. Complete Email and mobile verification. Please note that Email and mobile verification can only be verified by email and mobile phone number registered with Buyaladdin.

8. Click 'Request' to complete the transaction.


Note that a transaction fee will be charged, and ensure that you have sufficient funds to cover the fee.

Can I use other currencies in Buyaladdin while an ABBC withdrawal request is in progress?

After placing a 'Withdrawal' request for ABBC, you can use other currencies within Buyaladdin. It's important to note that the additional 'Withdrawal' request is unavailable until the previous request is complete.
However, you can still access your other cryptocurrencies and the 'Deposit' function while waiting for your request to be processed.

Will ABBC be valued at $100 outside of Buyaladdin?

No, ABBC will not be valued at $100 outside of Buyaladdin. If you withdraw your ABBC, its value will return to its current market price.
