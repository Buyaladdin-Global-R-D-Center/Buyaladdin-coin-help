Tether

Tether is the exclusive currency that is used for making purchases.

Is it possible to ‘Resend’ USDT(Tether) to my wallet?

Yes, 'Send' USDT will be sent to the external wallet by approval process of Buyaladdin.
Once you request ‘Send’, the transaction fee will be charged. Therefore, if amount you have is less than the amount of sending plus the fee, it will be impossible to 'Send' the amount.
Once you request ‘Send’, it may take 24 to 48 hours due to the approval process.
After you got approved, the amount you requested will be automatically sent to the wallet address.

Is there a user guide for ‘Send’?

Please refer to the following below.
Click 'Send' button to send USDT you have in Buyaladdin to your cryptocurrency wallet.
Once you request 'Send', please click 'Agree' button on the approval pop-up page according to the approval process of Buyaladdin.
Enter Cryptocurrency Wallet Address to send. (QR code is available)
Enter the amount of USDT. Dollar will be automatically calculated and displayed.
Please enter your email address and complete the verification. Registered email with Buyaladdin can only be verified.
Once you click ‘Send’ button, the requested amount will be sent to the wallet address.

Can I use the other currency after ‘Send’ request?

Yes, please note that when you request to ‘Send’ USDT, the additional ‘Send’ request is unavailable until the previous request is completed. (It is possible to use other currency.)
