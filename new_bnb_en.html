BNB

To build Global Integrated Payment Platform, Buyaladdin has completed adopting the BNB payment method within the Buyaladdin app.

Why was Binance Coin (BNB) added to Buyaladdin's payment system?

Buyaladdin has integrated Binance Coin (BNB) into its app as part of its efforts to establish a Global Integrated Payment Platform. The aim is to provide people from all over the world with a seamless and hassle-free shopping experience, regardless of their nationality or country.

You can now use Binance Coin (BNB) to purchase products from shopping malls in Korea through the Buyaladdin App.

How can I receive BNB in my Buyaladdin wallet?

With Buyaladdin, you can receive Binance Coin (BNB) from personal wallets or other exchanges directly into your BNB card.
Please keep in mind that the value of BNB is subject to real-time market prices and fluctuations.


You can receive amounts as small as 0.01BNB, with up to 8 decimal places of visibility and no maximum limit. Once you receive BNB from your wallet, you can use the amount reflected on your BNB card.


We recommend sending an amount greater than the minimum set by Buyaladdin to ensure the successful receipt of your requested amount.

What are the steps for receiving BNB in Buyaladdin?

To receive BNB on Buyaladdin, follow these steps:


1. Click ‘Receive’ on the ‘My Wallet’ page.

2. Agree to the related cryptocurrency agreement.

3. Agree to the BNB agreement and user guide.

4. Set your passcode if it’s your first time using the ‘Receive’ function.

5. After setting your passcode, you’ll be given your BNB wallet address. Copy and store the master key.

6. Return to the ‘My Wallet’ page and click ‘Receive’ again.

7. Choose whether to send from a wallet or exchange on the ‘Request to Receive’ page.

8. Enter the send date, send time, and amount to send. Note that sending less than the minimum amount may result in difficulty receiving the requested amount.

9. Click “Ok” to continue to the ‘Wallet QR’ page.

10. Send BNB from your exchange or wallet using the wallet address provided on the ‘Wallet QR’ page.

11. The amount you sent will be displayed in the app. Check for the latest transaction on the ‘My Wallet’ page.

12. Once the ‘receive’ is complete, you’ll receive a confirmation via email or in-app notification.

Can I send Binance Coin (BNB) from Buyaladdin to my personal wallet?

Yes, you can. To send BNB to other exchanges or personal wallets, you need to go through Buyaladdin's approval process, which may take 24 to 48 hours to complete.


Please note that a transaction fee will be charged when you make a 'Send' request. If the total amount you have is not sufficient to cover the transaction fee, the transaction may not be processed.


Once the 'Send' request is approved, the requested amount will be sent to the wallet address you entered.

What are the steps for sending BNB from Buyaladdin?

To send BNB from Buyaladdin to your cryptocurrency wallet, follow these steps:


1. Click the 'Send' button and go through Buyaladdin's approval process.

2. Click 'Agree' on the pop-up page.

3. Enter the wallet address manually or use the provided QR code.

4. Enter the amount of BNB. The dollar value will be automatically displayed.

5. Verify your email address registered in Buyaladdin.

6. Click 'Request' to complete the transaction.


Note that a transaction fee will be charged, and ensure that you have sufficient funds to cover the fee.

Can I use other currencies in Buyaladdin while a BNB send request is in progress?

After placing a 'Send' request for BNB, you can use other currencies within Buyaladdin. It's important to note that the additional 'Send' request is unavailable until the previous request is complete. However, you can still access your other cryptocurrencies and the 'receive' function while waiting for your request to be processed.
