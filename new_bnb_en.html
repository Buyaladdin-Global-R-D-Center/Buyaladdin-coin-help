Is there a reason why Binance Coin(BNB) has been added to the payment system in Buyaladdin?

To build Global Integrated Payment Platform, Buyaladdin has completed adopting the Binance Coin(BNB) payment method within the Buyaladdin app.
In addition, Buyaladdin is now out in force to build a durable system to make people around the world to purchase products simply, conveniently, and without any difficulties regardless of his/her nationality and country.

How can I ‘Receive’ BNB to Buyaladdin’s BNB wallet?

You can receive your Binance Coin(BNB) on other Exchanges or personal wallets into the BNB card within the Buyaladdin app.
Buyaladdin sets the value of Binance Coin(BNB) based on real-time market prices. Also, the price may change continuously.


You can receive in units of 0.01BNB, and the maximum receiving amount is unlimited. Like Bitcoin, it will show up to 8 decimal places. It can be used after receive BNB from your cryptocurrency wallet, and once the receive request is successfully completed, the amount that you have will show up on the Binance Coin(BNB) card.


Please send more than the minimum amount set by Buyaladdin. It will be difficult to receive the amount if you send less than the minimum amount.

Is there a user guide for BNB‘Receive’?

please refer to the following below.


Click the ‘Receive’ button on 'Wallet’ page.

Check the Agreement related to the cryptocurrency and click the ‘Agree’ button.

Check the ABBC, BTC, ETH and BNB Agreement & User Guide and click the ‘Agree’ button.

Please set your passcode if it is your first time to use ‘Receive’ function.

When you complete to set your Passcode, you will be given the ABBC,BTC, ETH and BNB wallet addresses. Please copy and store the masker key.

Click the 'Receive' button once more on ‘Wallet’ page.

Select where to send it from on the ‘Request to Receive’ page. (select ‘wallet’ or ‘exchange’)

Fill out the send date, send time, and the amount you want to send and click the ‘OK’ button. (It will be difficult to receive the amount if you send less than the minimum amount.

You will be automatically moved to ‘Wallet QR’ page.

Send ABBC, BTC,ETH and BNB from the exchange or wallet you use referring the ‘Wallet QR’ page.

The amount you sent will be displayed. Check the latest transaction on ‘My Wallet’ within the Buyaladdin.

When the ‘receive’ is complete, the receive confirmation will be sent to your email registered in Buyaladdin or the notification in the app.

Is it possible to ‘Send’ Binance(BNB) to my wallet?

Yes, To ‘Send’ BTC to other exchanges or personal wallets needs the Buyaladdin’s approval process.


When you request to ‘Send’, the transaction fee will be charged. Accordingly, if the amount you have is less than the amount of sending plus the fee, it will be difficult to ‘Send’ the amount.


When you request to ‘Send’, the ‘Send’ completion may take 24 to 48 hours due to the approval process.


After the ‘Send’ completion, the amount you requested will be sent to the wallet address you entered.

Is there a user guide for BNB ‘Send’?

please refer to the following below.


Click 'Send' button to send BNB you have within the Buyaladdin back to your cryptocurrency wallet.

You can ‘Send’ BNB according to the Buyaladdin’s approval process. Click the ‘Agree’ button on the approval pop-up page.

Enter the Wallet Address to send. (QR code is available)

Enter the amount of BNB. KRW will be automatically calculated and displayed.

Now, complete the email verification. Please enter your email address registered in Buyaladdin.

When you click the ‘Send’ button, the requested amount will be sent to the wallet address you entered.

Can I use the other currency within Buyaladdin after ‘Send’ request?

Yes, please note that when you request to ‘Send’ BNB, the additional ‘Send’ request is unavailable until the previous request is complete. (the use of ‘Receive’ and other currency you have within the app is available)
